About Us

We’re Neck Down Generation, a Placement & educational consultancy that loves to help, collaborate, and assist students. "We believe that Education gives you the Freedom to Rule the World." Chose the right Carrier path is very important because education is your attitude and it makes them able to meet their full potential and contribute to their society. We began our journey in 2020, it is just two men and an old motorcycle. In that Pandemic situation (COVID19) we find the number of unemployed people is increased day by day, we had decided to help people but not by the money, food, clothes but in a different way by the right carrier and job placement. And that day we build the company Neck Down Generation. Our vision is to help every individual student sincerely to find the best solutions for higher education. With years of experience in the field of college admission, we understand the pain points of our clients. To help you overcome these problems we have built collaborations with most of the top institutions for direct admission. As a Placement Consultancy, our vision is Employability enhancement. We understand the problems of employee and employer. We want to work as a bridge between them. Our mission is to "Choose the best from best". One day it will be the largest recruitment & education consultancy in the world.
